# Data from: Taxonomic corrections and new records for vascular plants of Kyrgyzstan, 6

[https://doi.org/10.5061/dryad.x3ffbg7wt](https://doi.org/10.5061/dryad.x3ffbg7wt)

## Description of the data and file structure

The records include the locality data, with a hierarchical attribution to province, country and continent, and also the elevation data in meters and the habitat information when available. The collection information is detailed to collector names (recordedBy) with field numbers when available, and collection dates. The source of documentation (basisOfRecord) is specified for each record as herbarium specimen or human observation. The locality data have been georeferenced either in the field or postfactum on the basis of the available information. Due to the natural data deficiency in some historical specimen labels, some values may be unavailable and coded as empty cells in the dataset.

Institution codes denote the museums in which the herbarium specimens are stored, and correspond to herbarium acronyms according to Index Herbariorum ([https://sweetgum.nybg.org/science/ih/)](https://sweetgum.nybg.org/science/ih/\)) for herbarium specimens; otherwise, these codes denote online resources from which the data have been harvested for evaluation: Plantarium ([https://www.plantarium.ru/](https://www.plantarium.ru/)) and iNaturalist ([https://www.inaturalist.org/)](https://www.inaturalist.org/\)). Museum catalogue numbers are provided for herbarium specimens when available.

The occurrence data are linked to accepted taxa. The taxonomic information is detailed to family, genus and species, sometimes also to subspecies or variety, with the indication of the nomenclatural authority (scientificNameAuthorship) and taxon rank. The taxonomic authority (identifiedBy) and the date of taxonomic verification (dateIdentified) is provided for each record. 

## Code/software

The data have been collected and processed in MS Excel software.
